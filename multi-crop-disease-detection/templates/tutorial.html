How to Detect Diseases in 5 Easy Steps

1

Capture Clear Images

Take photos of leaves against a plain background in good lighting. Ensure the entire leaf is visible and focus on affected areas.

  • Use natural daylight if possible
  • Keep the camera steady
  • Capture multiple angles
2

Upload Your Image

Click the upload button or drag and drop your image. You can also use your mobile camera directly from the website.

  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, JPEG
  • Maximum file size: 16MB
  • Both healthy and diseased leaves accepted
3

AI Analysis

Our advanced machine learning model analyzes the image patterns to identify potential diseases.

  • Pattern recognition for 50+ diseases
  • 95%+ accuracy rate
  • Real-time processing
4

Review Results

Get detailed diagnosis including disease name, confidence level, causes, and prevention methods.

  • Comprehensive disease information
  • Organic and chemical treatment options
  • Prevention strategies
5

Take Action

Follow the recommended treatments and monitor your crops regularly. Download or print the report for reference.

  • Immediate treatment suggestions
  • Long-term prevention plans
  • Regular monitoring advice

Pro Tips for Better Detection

Image Quality Tips
  • Clean the leaf surface before photographing
  • Use maximum resolution available
  • Avoid shadows and reflections
  • Include a scale reference if possible
Sampling Guidelines
  • Sample multiple leaves from different plants
  • Include both healthy and affected areas
  • Collect samples at different growth stages
  • Note environmental conditions
